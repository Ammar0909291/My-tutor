# The Student Digital Twin

The complete learner representation, assembled: the eight standing
dimensions (01–09) plus the momentary state (`../decision-engine/02`).
This file defines what the assembled twin must be able to answer, how
its answers carry their reasons, and the audit that proves one
architecture serves every learner.

## 1. The eight questions the twin must answer

| Question | Answered by |
|---|---|
| **What does this learner know?** | knowledge ladder positions, memory-modulated (02 + 07), per domain, with the frontier and floor reports |
| **What do they misunderstand?** | the misconception ledger (03): entries, strengths, statuses — competing knowledge listed separately from absent knowledge, always |
| **What scares them?** | the emotional model's triggers and imported history (06), with evidence and per-subject repair-path positions |
| **What motivates them?** | drivers and enjoyment sources (06 §4), curiosity style and hooks (05 §6), momentum state (09 §5) |
| **Which teaching actions work?** | affinity statistics (05 §3) + the teaching history's landed explanations, best demos, breakthroughs (08) |
| **Which consistently fail?** | the history's failed analogies, excluded families, deep-rung escalations (08 §1, `../decision-engine/04` filter 5's data) |
| **What should the tutor do next?** | the readiness derivation (09 §6) + the Decision Engine consuming all of the above — the twin informs; the engine decides |
| **Why?** | decision provenance (§2) — every answer above arrives with its evidence attached |

## 2. Decision provenance — the "why" made mandatory

Every decision the engine makes over this twin must be expressible as a
chain of twin entries: *"consolidate, don't advance — because:
slow-but-correct on fraction comparison this session (weather), FRAGILE
memory status on fraction-equivalence (07), chronic correct-but-afraid
profile in mathematics (04), and the affect budget is personal-1 (06
§2)."* The provenance rule exists for three reasons: it makes decisions
auditable (Part 9's standing audit needs it), it makes the model
falsifiable (a decision that worked/failed credits/debits the entries
that drove it — LAW 5's hypothesis updating), and it is the difference
between retrieval and invention: an unexplainable decision is an
invented one, by definition (README's retrieval claim, enforced).

## 3. The audit — one architecture, six learners (Part 11)

Can the SAME structure represent them all? Run each through the eight
dimensions:

- **A five-year-old complete beginner**: ladder all UNKNOWN (honest
  blanks, 01 §3); ledger empty; confidence profile = behavioral signals
  only (04, `../assessment/04 §2` age rule); behaviour profile carries
  the load — attention span in minutes, modality = enactment-heavy,
  persistence small; emotional model: no imported history yet — the
  entry says PROTECT (installing none is the job,
  `../first-lesson/01 §6`); trajectory empty, momentum tiny and
  precious. Fits — the model's blanks are doing honest work.
- **An adult beginner (reading at 40)**: ladder low but NOT zero —
  informal competence recorded as anchors; imported shame dominant in
  06 with dignity rules active; behaviour profile: vast life-knowledge
  = analogy goldmine flag; false-beginner watch active
  (`../first-lesson/01 §4`) so rungs update fast on recognition
  cascades. Fits.
- **A gifted learner**: high velocity and acceleration (09) driving
  compaction offers; ladder positions leaping rungs on gate-grade
  evidence only (02's law 1 prevents assumption-driven skipping);
  emotional model carries the load: fragile easy-identity watch,
  deliberate-early-struggle scheduling (Delivery 2 §9). The twin's
  insight: giftedness lives mostly in TRAJECTORY and EMOTION, not in
  the knowledge dimension — the ladder just moves faster. Fits.
- **A fearful learner**: 06 dominates — triggers enumerated, budget
  personal-1, repair-path positions per subject; 04 chronic
  correct-but-afraid with the record-showing intervention tracked;
  every other dimension operates under 06's constraints (the priority
  bands, `../decision-engine/02 §2`, made durable). Fits.
- **Returning after years**: high-water marks preserved vs modulated
  rungs (02 §2 law 3, 07 §2) — the twin distinguishes rusty from gone
  structurally; RECOVERED statuses expand fast; trajectory restarts
  with cascade-unlock expectations. Fits — and only because the ladder
  keeps two values, which the audit confirms as load-bearing.
- **A repeated-misconception learner**: the ledger is the star —
  regrowth counts, per-learner regression re-rating (03 §3), repair
  histories preventing burned collision reuse; trajectory's regression
  routing (09 §4) checks the ledger before blaming memory. Fits.

**Audit verdict**: the same eight dimensions serve all six; learner
types are REGIONS of the model space (different field values, different
dominant dimensions), not different architectures. No type-specific
structure was needed — which is the design's proof: a model that needed
a "gifted variant" or an "adult variant" would have been a persona
system wearing a model's clothes.

**Honest limits, recorded**: the twin does not diagnose learning
disabilities (dyslexia, dyscalculia, ADHD) — it will SURFACE their
signatures (voice-required failures, extreme forgetting rates, attention
data) as patterns worth professional attention, flagged for a human,
never named as diagnoses; it does not model family/context beyond what
the learner volunteers; and it holds one learner — peer dynamics are
out of scope. These are boundaries, not gaps: a tutor model that
pretended to clinical judgment would violate its own LAW 4 at the worst
possible place.

## 4. The twin in one paragraph (the retrieval endgame)

When the runtime opens a session, it retrieves: who this learner is
(eight dimensions, evidence-backed, decayed, per-domain), how they are
right now (the weather read, tuned by the climate), what the curriculum
offers next (the KG), what is known about teaching those concepts (the
concept entries), and how to decide (the Decision Engine). What it may
invent is wording and warmth in the moment. Everything else — who,
what, when, how, why — is authored, retrieved, and improving weekly
through the feedback loop. That is the moat: not that the AI is clever,
but that it is never guessing about the child in front of it.
